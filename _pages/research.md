My research explores AI systems that can perceive complex environments, explain their decisions, and act effectively in the physical or interactive world.

Research Directions
======

**Computer Vision.** I am interested in visual perception, representation learning, 3D/4D scene understanding, and vision systems that support downstream reasoning and action.

**Interpretable Learning.** I work on learning methods whose predictions, internal structure, and failure modes can be analyzed by researchers and practitioners.

**Agentic AI.** I study AI agents that combine perception, reasoning, memory, planning, and tool use to solve tasks over multiple steps.

**Robotics.** I am interested in connecting learned perception and decision making with embodied systems, including robotic manipulation, navigation, and interaction.
