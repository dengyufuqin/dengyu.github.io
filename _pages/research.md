My research explores AI systems that can perceive complex environments, explain and repair their decisions, and act effectively in the physical or interactive world.

Research Directions
======

**Computer Vision and 3D Perception.** I am interested in visual perception, representation learning, object segmentation, tracking, re-localization, and 3D scene understanding.

**Robotics and Visuomotor Control.** I work on perception-driven policies for manipulation and embodied agents, with an emphasis on geometric consistency and closed-loop control.

**Interpretable and Agentic AI.** I study systems that combine perception, reasoning, memory, planning, and tool use while keeping policy knowledge inspectable and repairable.
